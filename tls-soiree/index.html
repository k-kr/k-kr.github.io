Empower-Mint Mule

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 1/2 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 6-8 Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Ginger Beer (to top up)
  • Ice Cubes
  • Lime wedge and mint sprig (for garnish)

Instructions

  • In a copper mug or a highball glass, muddle the mint leaves gently to release their flavor.
  • Fill the mug with ice cubes.
  • Pour in the vodka and fresh lime juice.
  • Top up with ginger beer.
  • Stir gently to mix the ingredients.
  • Garnish with a lime wedge and a sprig of mint.

Gilhooly Gimlet

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1/2 oz Simple Syrup
  • Ice Cubes
  • Lime wheel (for garnish)

Instructions

  • Combine vodka, fresh lime juice, and simple syrup in a shaker with ice.
  • Shake well until chilled.
  • Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  • Garnish with a lime wheel.

Abandon the Checklist Chocolate Mattini

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Chocolate Liqueur
  • 1/2 oz Irish Cream
  • Ice
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder for garnish

Instructions

  • Fill a shaker with ice.
  • Add vodka, chocolate liqueur, and Irish cream to the shaker.
  • Shake well until the outside of the shaker feels cold.
  • Strain the mixture into a chilled martini glass.
  • Garnish with chocolate shavings or a dusting of cocoa powder.

Pivotal Patreon Paloma

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1/2 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • Grapefruit Soda (to top up)
  • Ice
  • Lime wheel or grapefruit slice for garnish
  • Salt for rim (optional)

Instructions

  • If using salt, rim a highball glass with it.
  • Fill the glass with ice.
  • Pour in the tequila and fresh lime juice.
  • Top up with grapefruit soda.
  • Stir gently to mix.
  • Garnish with a lime wheel or grapefruit slice.

Hundredth Episode Hooch

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Bourbon or Whiskey
  • 1/2 oz Maple Syrup
  • 1/2 oz Lemon Juice
  • Sparkling Apple Cider (to top up)
  • Ice
  • Apple slice and cinnamon stick for garnish

Instructions

  • In a shaker, combine bourbon or whiskey, maple syrup, and lemon juice with ice.
  • Shake well until the mixture is chilled.
  • Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice.
  • Top up with sparkling apple cider.
  • Garnish with an apple slice and a cinnamon stick.

Mattarita

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 4 oz Premade Margarita Mix
  • Ice
  • Salt (for rimming the glass)
  • Lime wedge (for garnish)

Instructions

  • Rim the edge of a margarita glass with lime and dip into salt to coat the rim.
  • Fill the glass with ice.
  • In a shaker, combine tequila and premade margarita mix with ice.
  • Shake well until the mixture is chilled.
  • Strain into the prepared glass.
  • Garnish with a lime wedge.
